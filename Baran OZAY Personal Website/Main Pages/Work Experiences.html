Uber Eats
When I first arrived in Melbourne, I worked as a food delivery driver for six months to explore the city's energy and diversity. This period gave me the opportunity to travel around different neighborhoods in Melbourne and work at new places every day. During my time in this sector, I got to know the city in depth by delivering food to various restaurants and cafes, and I met a lot of people. This experience allowed me to understand the social fabric of the city and observe the local lifestyle more closely. At the same time, I developed my communication skills and improved my ability to move quickly. Seeing the different faces of Melbourne and serving a broad range of customers were among the important experiences I gained during this time.

Bartending at the Melbourne Cricket Ground
Melbourne Cricket Ground is quite important to Melbourne's identity as a sports capital, contributing to its cultural, social, and economic life. I began working as a bartender here in March 2023. At this prestigious venue, I gained in-depth knowledge about cocktails, wine varieties, and various spirits. I had the opportunity to provide high-quality service to customers, and this experience led to significant professional and personal growth. The work environment offered a rich experience with its high-paced events and diverse customer profile. I love my job, and the time spent here has played a crucial role in developing my bartending skills. Working at this impressive and central location in the city has been a turning point in my career.

Kitchen Work at a Fast Food Restaurant
Since May 2023, I have been working in the kitchen of a fast food restaurant. In this position, I had the opportunity to experience the challenges and dynamics of working in a high-paced environment. During my time in the kitchen, I developed my ability to provide quick and effective solutions in crisis situations. Additionally, I improved my skills in communicating effectively with customers and significantly increased my work speed. Despite the intense pace, I find this job enjoyable, and the new challenges I face each day push me further professionally. This experience in the fast food industry has greatly enhanced my practical skills in the kitchen and my ability to adapt to the fast-paced work environment.
